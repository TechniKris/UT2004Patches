## Unreal Tournament 2004 Version 3374 Release Notes [ First Previews Available Soon! ]

Version 3374 is completely network compatible with all previous public releases of UT2004 (down to 3180).
Mods that were developed for older versions of UT2004 should also continue to work in this version.
If you are a mod author and you are having trouble getting your mod to work with the new version, then please come talk to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/thURucxzs6).
